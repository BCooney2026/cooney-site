How We Run

Our trucks are warehouses.

Most distributors run on pre‑orders and delivery windows. If you miss the window, you wait. We never worked that way. Every Cooney truck rolls loaded with live inventory, five days a week, in every county we cover. You run short at eleven, you can be slicing it by lunch.

  • No order windows. Buy the product the same day you need it.
  • Walk on and pick. Chefs and owners come onto the truck and choose product themselves.
  • Catering rescue. A party doubles on Friday afternoon and you still make it.
  • Recover from mistakes. A short count on Monday isn't a lost week.
  • Less money sitting in your cooler. You don't have to over‑buy to feel safe.

It costs us more to run this way. It has since day one. It's the reason accounts stay.

Before there was a Boar's Head market in this region, there were Cooney trucks on the road building one — Jim Thorpe to Wildwood. The territory got bigger and we handed pieces of it off. What we never handed off was how the job gets done.
